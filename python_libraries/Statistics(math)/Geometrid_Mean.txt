-------------------------------------- Geometric Mean ---------------------------------------
if x1,x2,x3....xn then,
 Geometric Mean (GM) = (x1.x2.x3....xn)^⅟n

Q) find the Geometric Mean of 2,4,8,16
ans -> (2*4*8*16)^⅟4
       (2^1*2^2*2^3*2^4)^1/4
       (2^1+2+3+4)^1/4
       (2^10)^1/4
       2^10/4
       2^5/2


---------------------------------------- harmonic Mean ----------------------------------------
if x1,x2,x3....xn then,
 harmonic Mean(HM) = 1/1/n(1/x1+1/x2+1/x3+...+1/xn)
